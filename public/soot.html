SOOT, the asset library that files itself.

Product design for a tool that sorts your work by what's inside it.

Selected screens

Every hard drive is a graveyard of final_v3_REALLY.png. We make more files than we can ever name, and naming was always the wrong job to hand a human.

SOOT sorts assets by what's actually in them. Designing it meant inventing a category with no precedent — nobody had drawn the primitives yet. So I drew them: Liquid Metal, a parts library loose enough to prototype any surface fast, and the Z index, a way to stack interface in depth instead of pretending the screen is flat.

The best filing system is the one you never have to think about.
