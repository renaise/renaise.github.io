Flora, a node canvas for the people who make images.

Zero-to-one product design for a node-based AI creative tool.

Selected screens

A node-based creative tool with AI at the center, and no map — no competitor to copy, no convention to break. The team had to know what it was before it could say what it did.

I shaped the editor around the people making images, not the ones wiring graphs: community models, generation, and curation on a single canvas that never assumes you can code. The depth of a node graph, without making anyone learn one.

Give people the power and hide the wiring. That's the whole job.
