Lighthouse, recasting immigration as a beacon for talent.

An identity for immigration as a hiring advantage, not a hardship to survive.

Selected screens

Most immigration products sell relief from paperwork. They meet people at their most anxious and promise to make the dread shorter. Lighthouse sells the opposite end of the same story — immigration as how a company wins the people everyone else is chasing.

I directed and designed the identity up from its 1.0: elevated, enterprise-grade, pitched to the HR leaders quietly staffing global teams. American excellence offered as a reason to come build the future here, rather than a gauntlet to get through.

A border can read as a wall or a beacon. The brand decides which.
