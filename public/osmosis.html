Osmosis AI, training small models to beat the giants.

Brand and website for an applied reinforcement-learning company.

Selected screens

Osmosis runs on an unfashionable bet: a small model trained hard on one task beats a frontier model renting you general intelligence you'll never use, at a fraction of the cost.

I built the brand and site to make post-training feel like a frontier rather than a config panel — a gradient sphere, Exposure set against Neue Haas Grotesk and PX Grotesk, an ink-wash mountain range under "Today Teaches Tomorrow." Cobalt-on-black that reads closer to a film title than a dev tool.

The science is dry. The promise isn't. The brand had to carry the second one.
