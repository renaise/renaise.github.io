Flora AI — product design notes.

A working teardown of the node canvas: the design framework, the model strategy, and the orchestration layer that ties creative workflows together.

Three questions ran through the build — how the tool should feel, what it should be built on, and how it should compose. The notes below organize the thinking into those three layers.

01

Design

Jan – Mar 2024
Framework

“Flora AI” is a superpowered paintbrush and easel. Flora is the network of artists. Neo-Renaissance is the movement.

LEGO-ifying pipelines and blocks.

Slowness as an aesthetic principle.

  • Tech that helps us get back to slowness, stillness, and contemplation.
  • Antithetical to the mandate of tech.

Each node will have a form of loading.

Custom controlGenerative
  • Every TD node has noise — you can use that for generation.
  • Speak 2D into existence.
  • What can yield the best result for a designer?

Langchain — vector databases, RAG.

03

Models

Yes, this is a wrapper…
Strategy

Foundation models — companies typically build these stronger. A wrapper around existing models.

Moat — unique data you can gather to fine-tune smaller models to make your business better?

Relying too much on foundation models is dangerous.

  • Building on top of other companies' APIs can be detrimental.
  • Discern what the trends are going to be — build your own moat.

What is the AI model improvement journey?

Technical surface area:

FAL abstraction · 7 parameters SDXL Turbo · trained on 512×512 Nodes · Stable Diffusion
04

Orchestration Layer

Creative-friendly workflows
System

Preview export node: running “npm” Flora on any .proj file.

Computing as art and memory ecosystem.

Modular networks — what is the most usable, extensible form of data?

  • As approachable as JSON.
  • As usable as a spreadsheet.
  • As extensible as a relational database.
  • As distributable as RSS.

Embodied computing.

  • Easy persistence / persistent collaborative software.
  • Emergent syntax — whole data, where the data is in the representation of it.
