MediumIdentity + Web
Timeline2025
Context

Sensitive Subjects makes photoreal images of products and technology — including the chip, the rig, the concept that doesn't physically exist yet — on an AI-accelerated pipeline that turns weeks of renders into days. Each one lands on-brand and ready to run: an ad, a pitch, a brand refresh.

Training small models to beat the giants

ClientOsmosis AI
MediumBrand + Web
Timeline2025
Context

Osmosis runs on an unfashionable bet: a small model trained hard on one task beats a frontier model renting you general intelligence you'll never use, at a fraction of the cost.

Outcome

I built the brand and site to make post-training feel like a frontier rather than a config panel — a gradient sphere, Exposure set against Neue Haas Grotesk and PX Grotesk, an ink-wash mountain range under "Today Teaches Tomorrow." Cobalt-on-black reads closer to a film title than a dev tool. That's the point: the science is dry, the promise isn't.

08Independent · Lab

ai://www.

Teaching an LLM to reach the live web

ClientSelf-initiated · w/ Shovon Hasan
MediumSpeculative Thesis
Timeline2024
Context

An LLM can describe how to book a flight but can't reach the booking page. The question was blunt — how does a language model touch a live website when no one has built it an app to do so?

Outcome

The model writes an HTTP request as plain text, the browser runs it, and the real site renders inside the chat window. Sites publish a /mythra.txt that tells the model what they can do — a robots.txt for actions instead of crawlers.

09Independent · Stage

Rememe / Collage

Remixing memes on a composable canvas

ClientCollage
MediumMobile App (iOS)
Timeline2022
Context

Collage launched in April 2022 as "the meme app" — page admins, campus groups, deranged-meme tools, a leaderboard ranking the "top collagers." A feed built to win at memes.

Outcome

Rememe rebuilt it around a single verb: to rememe a post is to remake it. The feed shows who rememed yours and ranks by how often it's been remade; profiles open up freeform. The roasts and the leaderboard gave way to a lo-fi room where reach is counted in remixes, not likes.
