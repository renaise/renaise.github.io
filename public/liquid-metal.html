Liquid Metal, recombine, don't redraw.

The design library SOOT is built and rebuilt from.

Selected screens

SOOT shipped new surfaces every week. A pile of one-off screens means redrawing the same button forever — and watching the product drift out of sync with itself.

I made Liquid Metal: parts you recombine instead of redraw, loose enough to prototype anything SOOT needed next. Then I ran the brand operations that kept it coherent as the product multiplied.

A design library is a promise that the next screen will already feel like the last one.
